The Meta Creative Machine — One-Page SOP

Goal is NOT one winning ad. It's a LIBRARY of proven concepts that feeds your scaling campaign. Andromeda targets at the creative level — variety IS the targeting.

Variable 1 — Concept Variety

Persona × Awareness Matrix. 3–5 personas × 5 awareness stages. Each cell = a unique concept (different hook, language, objection — NOT different colors).
  • S1 Unaware — doesn't know they have a problem
  • S2 Problem Aware — knows problem, not solutions
  • S3 Solution Aware — knows solutions, not yours
  • S4 Product Aware — knows you, not convinced
  • S5 Most Aware — ready, needs a reason
Most advertisers only write S4–S5 (saturated, expensive). Writing S1–S3 unlocks a bigger, cheaper market.

Variable 2 — Format Variety

One format = a ceiling. Rotate ONE per week across the month:
  • Ugly Ads — text-on-image, 1200×1200, bold headline, "How to X without Y", price visible, high contrast
  • AI Ads — ChatGPT Image / Gemini / Higgsfield; before/after, text over visual
  • Lifestyle — native benefit photos; copy carries it (great for warm)
  • Meme — Drake / Two Buttons on real audience tension → shares → lower CPMs
  • Reels — selfie, vertical, 10–20s, headline overlay, IG original audio

The Weekly Operating System

1. Pick ONE format for the week (rotate monthly) 2. Pull 3–5 matrix cells — vary stages (≥1 early, ≥1 mid, ≥1 late) 3. One ad per cell = 5 concepts, same format
4. Write copy for THAT specific cell (the whole point) 5. Launch in testing campaign — NEW ads in NEW ad sets (2-Campaign Method) 6. Judge on CPA + AOV (not CTR). Winning post IDs → Campaign 2 (scale), loaded with variety
Compounding: ~1 mo = 20 ads / 4 formats / 20 cells  •  ~3 mo = matrix coverage competitors can't match.   Avoid: filling the whole matrix in all 5 formats week 1 → overwhelm → quit. One format, 3–5 cells, sustainable.

The 5 Laws Every Winner Obeyed

1. One IdeaOne pain, one promise, one action.
2. Specificity"$478K from 40K opt-ins" > "grow your business".
3. Sound HumanLike a post from a friend, not a company.
4. Proof = CopyScreenshots, numbers, transformations.
5. Sell the Next Step93% drive to free training/book/call, not the sale.

Copy Rules — sound spoken, not written

No three-word staccato bursts  •  No announcement phrases ("Here's the truth…", "Let me show you")  •  Data creates drama, not sentence structure — exact numbers in complete sentences  •  One idea per paragraph, max 2 lines  •  Isolate your strongest claim on its own line  •  Pair every logical reframe with emotional absolution ("It was never your fault.")  •  Kill AI-tells: delve, leverage, unlock, unleash, game-changer, seamless, robust, supercharge, dive deep, level up.
