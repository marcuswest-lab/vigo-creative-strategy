The operating system this doc applies

  • Variety IS the targeting. Andromeda ranks at the creative level — fill the Persona × Awareness matrix instead of chasing one winner.
  • Under-exploited stage. The cheapest untapped market is Problem Aware — men who feel the problem but haven't tied it to a treatable, prescribable cause.
  • One format per week (rotate monthly), 3–5 matrix cells, one ad per cell, change multiple signals to beat similarity clustering.
  • Judge on CPA + AOV, promote winning post IDs to the scaling campaign (2-Campaign Method).
  • Reference: One-Page SOP

⚠ Flags & research gaps (read before scaling)

  • Meta health compliance (critical): no implying the viewer personally has ED, no explicit before/after body claims, no shaming — follow Meta's health & personal-attribute rules. Guardrails are in the doc's Compliance section.
  • Unconfirmed price: the $198/mo figure came from the Parker persona / mined docs, not a live-page confirmation — verify before launch.
  • No performance data: the doc is built from offer/angle research, not ad-account results. The first-week batch is a set of hypotheses; re-weight once real CPA/ROAS lands.
